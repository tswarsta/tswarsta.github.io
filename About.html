About

Hello- My name is Treat. I am a designer, born on Halloween - hence my name. I am currently a junior attending Carnegie Mellon University studying communication design, I am also working on a minor in fine arts. Being born and growing up in such a fast pace and modern city that is Singapore has led me to believe that I have been exposed to a different way of life and style that I believe has helped fuel my design thinking.




I believe good design is clean design - simple; however, my fine art background has caused me to look further into the worlds of art and technology, which have led me to believe and become widely obsessed with bridging the minimalist aesthetic I am so interested in with traits most commonly found in the art field. This exploration has led to me think in a different way - that design, even though its main focus should be delivering the message it was intended to do, still can be and must be interesting to look at and not just a stark white background (which I do still love as you can see by my website).


My everyday experiences also play a huge role in influencing my work, this is my Instagram where I try my best to document and curate my everyday actions and interactions as well as visual cues that are currently inspiring me.
