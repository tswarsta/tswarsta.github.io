Home Energy Score

Many homes, specifically in Pennsylvania are not energy efficient. Our goal for this project - encourage home owners to obtain an energy audit.


Home Energy Score from Treat Swarstad on Vimeo.


My final piece is a video intended on being shared through social media, on websites, etc. It is meant for home owners who are unaware of problems in their home; basically it is meant to serve as a starter video for home owners, a taste of energy efficiency that will lead users into wanting to learn more about being more efficient.



Univers Type Exploration

For this project we were each assigned a typeface. I personally received Universe 39 Thin Ultra Condensed. Our task - to create an animation that captured our assigned typeface’s signature characteristics.


Univers Type Exploration from Treat Swarstad on Vimeo.


Univers is a san-serif designed by Adrian Frutiger. In order to concisely portray the essence of Univers I knew I would have to capture its simplistic style that had, “visual sensitivity between thick and thin strokes”, which characterizes Univers as a not strictly geometric typeface. I wanted to keep the video as simple as possible as Univers can be described as rather minimalistic, however, I still wanted to show how many facets it had and the amount of different weights and style available in the family. Depicting all the different utilizations of Univers was also important as in todays’ society it is one of the most popular typefaces.


Reflecting on my design, if I were to iterate further I would remove the extreme focus on the geometric quality Univers has and extend research into its curvature because Frutiger’s aim was not to create another geometric typeface, which were extremely popular during the era. but one that was not rigid and part of a common school of thought.
