Selected Photography

I believe there is a correlation within forms between manmade and natural objects; especially in today’s society where inspirations between these two realms are more and more apparent and large overlapping occur. The idea that the manmade has clear parallels to organic forms (1-10).


As an individual who is secluded from the collection, disposal and reproduction of waste – I decided that trash in the modern world, to the consumer, is invisible. Trash travels from the buyer to the trashcan and then “disappears”. The main message I wanted to present in this series of images is that consumers discard of trash without even thinking of the impact of their trash or where their trash goes, I wanted to show what would occur if trash was stored instead of disposed of – to display what would occur if landfills were nonexistent (11-16).
