INTENT

Surveillance has become a business model and we have become the product. Today, it is impossible to be disconnected; we are all part of the Internet – it is embedded into our routines and daily rituals. HIDING IN PLAIN SITE juxtaposes found articles with memes in order to comment on the discrepancy between the severe impacts of surveillance and how we have learnt to cope with it.
