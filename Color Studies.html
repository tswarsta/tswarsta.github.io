Fruit Packaging

For this project we were tasked with creating packaging for fruit and how the fruits essence could be used for inspiration.



My partner and I’s inspiration came from the literal essence of the fruit. We scanned the fruit as well as took photos of sliced fruit on a light board to come up with our “print”, which is of a grapefruit and a kiwi.




A zine we shot after the project was completed.

The Colors of Phipps

Inspiration and photographs taken at the nearby Phipps Conservatory - how can we use color to tell a story and to change the mood of an environment?



I wanted to utilize color to transport the viewer into a different environment. Phipps has many different rooms with plants from many different parts of the globe with many different temperatures. I wanted to take the user on a journey through temperatures and climate by using the lines and color of plants to tell my story.
